LuxuryCraft Rules and Guidelines

Rules and Guidelines are a really important part of the community and its development. This page will help you know whats best to do both on the Minecraft Server or the Discord Server.

Rules and Guidelines

  • You must respect other players.
  • Swearing is allowed to a point but if it exceeds the limits, it can result in a mute or ban.
  • You must not spam or advertise. Links can be sent to the chat. If players abuse this ability, they can be even be muted or banned.
  • Inviting Bots / Alt Accounts just to get rewards is permanently bannable
  • Asking for Staff can be ignored to a point but the player begging can also be banned or muted.
  • Asking Staff members to review your application can get your application denied automatically.
